Why this is harder than it looks
◐
Occlusion — limbs disappear behind the body mid-movement, and tracking has to hold up anyway.
◑
Domain gap — models trained in clean lab conditions tend to break down in a real bedroom or gym.
◒
Personalization — no two bodies move the same way, so rigid thresholds give false corrections.
